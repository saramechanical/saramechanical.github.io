About Us

Sara Mechanical Solutions (GSTIN No.09AAVPZ5852A1Z1) established in the year Jan 2018 with a group of industry experienced professionals. Our mission is to produce high quality and reliable products to the customer at best competitive rates. A well equipped machine shop is located in Saharanpur, Uttar Pradesh along with qualified inspectors and designers having experience from BHEL, Ordnance factory and Opto electronics factory. We render our maximum effort to fulfill customer requirement in achieving best quality, timely delivery with competitive cost. We welcome your interest and participation to be part of our company through which both of our organizations would grow. The company is equipped with all the latest design facilities for which there are qualified professionals employed. The professionals working are highly skilled in 2D and 3D drawings and having been working for decades, hence has good designing capability. They have been given dedicated computers which are equipped with designing software such as AutoCAD, Solidworks and Creo. Customer satisfaction is our primary goal and we are passionate to achieve it. We are continuously integrating your suggestions to serve you better. We would love to see dedicating your time for improving us in your own way by providing feedbacks..
